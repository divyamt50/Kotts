Welcome to KOTTS!

Born in Najibabad, India in 2018, KOTTS evokes the very spirit of the state it was founded in. An upstart, innovative, and dynamic brand – KOTTS – offers the best of contemporary, ethnic Indian fashion and fusion-wear styles. Staying true to the brand’s unique promise of premium and wearable fashion, KOTTS unveils fresh collections and all-new designs throughout the fashion calendar. KOTTS design and aesthetic sensibility seek inspiration from all walks of life - be it the beauty of nature and heritage in art, architecture and culture, intricate creations, and the modern world to the home-bound handloom traditions of India.

Kotts Pvt Ltd. is an Indian online clothing company based in Najibabad, India. Founded by Archana Tyagi, Abhay Tyagi, and Divyam Tyagi in 2018, the company initially focused on women's clothing and traditional wear before expanding into other product categories such as lifestyle products.

Quality Craftsmanship

At KOTTS, we take pride in our commitment to quality craftsmanship. Each piece of clothing is meticulously crafted to ensure superior fit, comfort, and durability.

Fashion Trends

Stay ahead of the fashion curve with KOTTS. Our designers keep a keen eye on the latest trends and create stylish, contemporary designs that are perfect for any occasion.

Ethical Practices

We are committed to ethical practices and sustainability. Our production processes prioritize fair trade, eco-friendly materials, and supporting local artisans and communities.
